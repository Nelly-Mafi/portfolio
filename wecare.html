ABOUT THE PROJECT

Design for Personalized Health

During university, I became fascinated with how designers could impact healthcare and biotechnology. My analysis of AI in Medicine and bioinformatics revealed a gap in user-centered design. For my graduation project, I demonstrated the potential of effective biotechnology products through interdisciplinary collaboration. Mehrdad Moosavi (cancer researcher and bioinformatician) offered valuable guidance, highlighting the innovative yet challenging aspects of our work. I was also mentored by Dr. Janessa Laskin (co-lead of the POG program), which significantly enriched the experience. This project stands out as one of the most rewarding of my career, providing crucial lessons in design thinking and collaboration. It was recognized with the ECU Health Design Award for Service, a testament to its impact.
